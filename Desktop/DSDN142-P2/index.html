FURTHER RESEARCH


INTERACTION GOAL

The goal of this interaction is to purchase and receive an order of coffee. This interaction includes the social aspect of ordering and interacting with another human being.

ELEMENTS INVOLVED

The elements involved in this interaction is the person, the employee, money and the coffee. The steps involving this interaction is waiting in line whilst deciding on your order, verbally communicating your order, paying for the drink and receving the coffee after waiting for it to be made.

INFLUENCES

Influences involving the ordering of the coffee differed for the individiual user. Some were influenced by the specials or new drink on the menu whilst others knew their exact order stright away based off of past experience. Each step in the process mattered in the ordering system as they influence the next step.

FINDINGS

Many users when surveyed found that the initial waiting process tedious and often grew impatient if there was a long line. However we found after they had ordered they were less likely to be as impatient as the interaction process is close to the end.
